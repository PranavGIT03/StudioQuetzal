Hospitality · Case Study 01

Radisson Hotel, Indore

Indore, Madhya Pradesh

The brief called for a complete overhaul of an existing hotel lighting scheme — one that had degraded in performance and no longer reflected the property's four-star positioning. We designed a DALI-controlled layered lighting system across all public areas, restaurants, and guestroom corridors.

Tunable white fixtures replaced fixed-colour sources, enabling the team to shift the lobby's atmosphere from energising morning tones to a warm, intimate evening character — without changing a single fixture. The outcome was a 35% reduction in energy consumption and a measurable improvement in guest satisfaction scores within the first quarter post-commissioning.

Commercial · Case Study 02

Phoenix Mall Atrium, Bhopal

Bhopal, Madhya Pradesh

Phoenix Malls approached us to redesign the primary atrium of their Bhopal development — a triple-height, skylit space that was simultaneously under-lit at night and energy-inefficient. Our scheme introduced a layered approach: high-output LED wallwashers for the structural frame, discreet downlights for the retail frontages, and automated daylight sensors that continuously optimise output against the prevailing natural light.

The result was a 60% energy saving against the previous system and a dramatically improved retail environment that tenant brands reported had a positive effect on footfall and dwell time in adjacent units.

Retail · Case Study 03

Tanishq Boutique, Indore

Indore, Madhya Pradesh

Jewellery retail demands the highest colour rendering performance of any lighting application. For Tanishq's Indore boutique, we specified a bespoke LED system with CRI 98+ sources — designed to render gold, diamonds, and gemstones with the accuracy that converts browsers into buyers.

Display cases were lit with precisely aimed micro-spotlights that eliminate shadows and highlight facets. The ambient scheme provides a warm, luxurious atmosphere without competing with the merchandise. The result was an interior that the brand team described as a benchmark for their regional portfolio.
