The Studio

A Design Practice
Built on Light

Studio Quetzal is Indore's premier architectural lighting design firm. Founded in 2015 by Amita Dubey, we combine photometric precision with a refined design sensibility — transforming built environments into experiences that resonate long after the first impression.

Our name draws from the vivid Quetzal bird, embodying our conviction that light should be vivid, precise, and transformative. We serve hospitality, commercial, residential, retail, and institutional clients across central India.

Why Studio Quetzal

Six Reasons Clients
Come Back to Us

01

Design-First Philosophy

Every project begins with the architecture, not the fixture list. Light is treated as a material — shaping space, mood, and perception from the very first concept sketch.

02

Technical Mastery

Our team holds deep expertise in photometrics, lumen calculations, control systems, and energy compliance — ensuring designs perform as beautifully as they look.

03

Collaborative Process

We integrate seamlessly with architects, interior designers, and MEP consultants from concept through commissioning, ensuring every discipline is aligned.

04

Energy Intelligence

Our solutions routinely deliver 35–60% energy savings through smart zoning, daylight harvesting, and DALI controls — without any compromise to design quality.

05

Bespoke Solutions

There are no template designs here. Every scheme is crafted specifically for the space, the client's brand, and the human experience we want to create.

06

End-to-End Service

From initial brief to final calibration, we handle every stage — concept, documentation, site supervision, and commissioning — as a single, accountable practice.
