Amita Dubey

Director & Principal Designer

Amita Dubey founded Studio Quetzal with a singular conviction: that lighting design in India deserved to be treated as a discipline in its own right - not an afterthought handled by electricians, but a creative and scientific practice integral to great architecture.

Over eleven years and more than 200 projects across central India, she has built a practice that sits at the intersection of photometric rigour and design artistry. Her leadership has made Studio Quetzal the go-to partner for developers, hoteliers, and institutions who understand that light defines experience.

"Life is too short for boring lighting. Give us a space and let us prove it."

Amita leads every project personally through the concept stage, ensuring that the studio's design intent is embedded deeply before technical execution begins.

Our Philosophy

Light as a Living Material

Our name is drawn from the Quetzal - one of the world's most brilliantly coloured birds, celebrated for its vivid precision and rarity. It perfectly captures what we believe light should be: vivid enough to transform, precise enough to serve the architecture, and rare enough in its execution to be memorable.

We believe that a well-lit space does more than look beautiful. It directs attention, shapes emotion, signals brand identity, supports wellbeing, and extends the life and usability of every environment we work in. This is the conviction that drives every brief we accept.

How We Work

Our Six-Stage
Design Process

A proven methodology that ensures creative ambition and technical discipline work hand in hand at every stage.

01

Discovery

We begin with an immersive site assessment and client briefing - understanding the architecture, the brand, the users, and the performance benchmarks that matter most.

02

Concept Design

We develop mood boards, zone strategies, and lighting narratives that translate your brief into a clear design vision - complete with fixture direction and atmospheric intent.

03

Design Development

Photometric calculations, luminaire specifications, control system design, and energy modelling - every technical dimension resolved before documentation begins.

04

Documentation

Complete tender-ready drawings, specifications, and installation guides - co-ordinated with the architect and MEP engineer to eliminate site ambiguity.

05

Site Support

Our designers are present during critical installation phases, ensuring that the design intent is preserved and that every fixture is positioned and aimed correctly.

06

Commissioning

Final calibration of every scene, zone, and control sequence - followed by client handover training and a post-occupancy review to confirm performance.
