Architectural Lighting

Architectural lighting is the practice of using light to reveal, celebrate, and extend the life of a building's design. We approach every facade, atrium, and interior sequence as a canvas — determining how natural and artificial light can work in concert from dawn to well past dusk.

Our designs achieve seamless day-to-night continuity, so the architectural intention reads with equal clarity under any condition. Where applicable, we incorporate LEED-compatible strategies that satisfy both sustainability benchmarks and design ambition.

  • Enhances visual impact and architectural legibility after dark
  • Creates coherent day-to-night continuity for facades and interiors
  • LEED-compatible approaches that meet sustainability targets
  • Precision beam control to minimise light pollution
Sample Projects
Prestige Tower, Indore Heritage Hotel Facade, Ujjain

Landscape Lighting

Great landscape lighting transforms outdoor environments into compelling nocturnal experiences - extending their utility, enhancing their natural character, and creating a sense of safety and wonder simultaneously.

We design for gardens, courtyards, promenades, and public spaces using layered strategies that highlight planting, water features, pathways, and architectural edges. Solar and low-energy options are available wherever the brief supports them.

  • Extends the usable hours of outdoor environments
  • Highlights natural features - trees, water, topography
  • Solar and low-voltage options for sustainable installations
  • Pathway and wayfinding systems integrated with the scheme
Sample Projects
Lotus Garden, Bhopal Riverfront Promenade, Indore

Commercial Lighting

Commercial Lighting

Commercial lighting must do several things at once: support productivity, reinforce brand identity, comply with international standards, and achieve aggressive energy targets. Our designs balance all of these without compromise.

We work across offices, banking halls, corporate campuses, and mixed-use developments — delivering smart-ready systems that can be adjusted, scheduled, and monitored remotely. Energy savings of up to 60% are routinely achieved versus legacy systems.

  • Boosts employee wellbeing through biophilic and circadian design
  • Reduces energy consumption by up to 60% vs. conventional systems
  • Smart-ready controls compatible with BMS and DALI protocols
  • Reinforces brand presence through deliberate focal lighting
Sample Projects
Axis Bank HQ, Indore Phoenix Mall Atrium, Bhopal

Retail Lighting
Expertise 04

Retail Lighting

In retail, light is a sales tool. The right illumination increases dwell time, improves product perception, guides movement through the store, and communicates brand values before a single word is spoken.

We design merchandise-focused schemes using high-CRI sources that render colour accurately, focal lighting that draws the eye, and adaptable systems that can transition between seasonal campaigns without a fixture change.

  • High-CRI LED sources for accurate colour rendering on merchandise
  • Strategic focal lighting to drive attention and purchasing intent
  • Scene-based adaptability for seasonal campaigns and promotions
  • Facade and window illumination to attract footfall
Sample Projects
Tanishq Boutique, Indore Lifestyle Store, Bhopal

Hospitality Lighting
Expertise 05

Hospitality Lighting

Hospitality lighting is the art of mood at scale. A luxury hotel needs to be dramatic in the lobby, intimate in the restaurant, energising in the gym, and deeply restful in the guestroom - often simultaneously. We design each zone with its own identity while maintaining a coherent brand narrative throughout.

Our hospitality schemes are built for longevity: circadian-friendly systems that support guest wellness, PMS-integrated controls that respond to room status, and robust luminaires that perform across years of intensive use.

  • Zone-by-zone mood design that creates a layered guest experience
  • Circadian-friendly systems to support guest wellbeing and sleep
  • PMS integration for automatic scene transitions with room status
  • Specified for longevity under demanding hospitality conditions
Sample Projects
The Grand Meridian Hotel, Indore Radisson Resort, Pachmarhi

Residential Lighting

Residential Lighting

A luxury home's lighting should feel inevitable — as if the space could not possibly be lit any other way. Our residential designs layer ambient, task, and accent sources to create schemes that shift gracefully between morning energy, daytime comfort, evening warmth, and late-night intimacy.

We work closely with interior designers on bespoke schemes for villas, penthouses, and high-end apartments — integrating fully with smart home ecosystems and specifying fixtures that are as beautiful unlit as they are when glowing.

  • Layered schemes adapting seamlessly to daily living rhythms
  • Full smart home integration with voice and app control
  • Accent lighting that celebrates artwork, architecture, and material
  • Energy monitoring and scene scheduling for effortless living
Sample Projects
Luxury Villa, Bhopal Penthouse at Malwa Residency, Indore
